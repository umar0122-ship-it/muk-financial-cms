Frequently asked questions

What happens on the first call?
A relaxed 30-minute conversation about your business, your current bookkeeping setup, and what's causing pain. You'll get honest first impressions and a recommended next step — even if that step isn't hiring us.
Do you work with businesses outside the US?
Our focus is US-registered entities (LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp, partnerships) and US tax filings. If your business is US-registered, your physical location doesn't matter.
How do you keep my financial data secure?
We use accountant-level access in QuickBooks/Xero (you stay the owner), secure document collection during onboarding, and never ask for banking passwords. Access can be revoked by you at any time.
How quickly can you start?
Onboarding typically begins within 2–3 days of a signed proposal, with your first deliverable inside the first week.
