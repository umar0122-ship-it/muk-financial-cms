Common questions

Pricing FAQ

What happens if my needs go beyond the listed scope?
Nothing dramatic. We flag it in your monthly review and adjust the quote only if the extra scope is consistent. One busy month never changes your bill.
Do I still need a CPA?
We prepare your returns and partner with licensed CPAs for review and filing sign-off — that review is included in your plan. If you already have a CPA you trust, we work alongside them and keep your books CPA-ready year-round.
My books are a mess. Can I start on a monthly plan right away?
Usually we start with a one-time cleanup project (quoted after a free review), then transition you to a monthly plan once the backlog is cleared. Most cleanups finish inside 3–6 weeks.
How do we communicate and share documents?
Your choice of email, Slack, or WhatsApp for day-to-day questions, with secure document collection during onboarding. Growth and Enterprise plans include priority response times.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. Plans run month-to-month after an initial 90-day engagement, and you keep full access to your own QuickBooks or Xero file at all times — it's your data.
