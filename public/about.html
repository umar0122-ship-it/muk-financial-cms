Our story

Banking discipline, applied to small-business finance

We built MUK Financial on a simple observation: small businesses deserve the same financial rigor that banks demand of their largest borrowers.

Why we exist

Most businesses don't fail from bad products. They fail from unclear numbers.

After 14+ years underwriting credit at global banks, our founder saw the same pattern in every declined loan file: capable owners, real revenue, and books that couldn't tell their story. MUK Financial exists to close that gap.

We combine institutional credit experience with hands-on US tax and accounting practice — so your financials are not just tidy, they're lender-grade, investor-grade, and audit-defensible.
